Fabio CRUZ1, Giovanny ARBELAEZ2 et Mauricio CAMARGO3

2021-01-22

Bonjour à tous !

We give you a warm welcome to the module of reproducible research for in ENSGSI master course.

We care for open science and open practices. Thus, this website is hosted on GitHub, a open source plateform, where you will find the source documents and holds all the practical instructions and data.
